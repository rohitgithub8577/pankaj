About Us

Pankaj Construction, established in 2013 and headquartered at Intiyathok, Gonda, is a premier infrastructure development company executing high-value government and private tenders across Uttar Pradesh and India. We combine technical expertise with unwavering integrity.

We specialize in EPC contracts for roads, highways, residential townships, and industrial structures. Our ISO-certified processes and skilled workforce ensure every project meets global standards.

Vision

To be the most trusted infrastructure partner contributing to nation building from the heart of Gonda.

Mission

Deliver excellence through innovation, safety, and sustainable practices.
